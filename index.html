Some random information

Starigrad - Croatia

This was my first vacation on the Croatian shores. I travelled around Starigrad and went all around the smaller and bigger cities, with my camera, it was a blast!

Zaragoza - Spain

This was my first trip to a city in Spain that was not Barcelona. My sister moved there with her boyfriend, so I had another excuse to take some wonderful photos and travel around.

Valencia - Spain

My second trip to Spain, where I had the opportunity to document one of the biggest rain storms in the country since the 80s. I still managed to take wonderful photos of the wet stormy city and the beautiful ocean museums and markets.

Amsterdam - Netherlands

One of my favorite trips with a bunch of my friends from childhood. We travelled around the capital city and explored its cute and wonderful locations.
